# Reviewer Simulator

## Trigger

Use for reviewer-style critique, pre-submission risk pass, or “what will reviewers attack?” when a broader review than CFD-only checklist is needed.

## Reviewer criteria

- objective and motivation clear
- well-placed in literature
- claims supported by evidence
- significant contribution
- reproducible methods
- limitations honest
- figures/tables support claims

## Decision risk

low / medium / high

## Most valuable revision under time constraint

## Anti-patterns

- Writing a friendly summary instead of a review with decision risk.
- Flagging generic ML weaknesses while missing CFD reproducibility details.
- Inventing reviewer concerns not grounded in the manuscript.
- Treating missing experiments as minor wording issues.

## Verification

- Quote evidence where available.
- Mark inference vs manuscript-proven fact.
- Do not invent details.
